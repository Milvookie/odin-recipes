Croque Monsieur

Description

A french classique.

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread (2 per croque monsieur)
  • Ham
  • Cheese (emmental or gruy√®re work best, but whatever float your boat)
  • Mustard
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Make the bechamel. In a pan, make a roux (flour and butter in a 1/1 ratio). Add milk little by little and mix well to avoid chunks.
  2. Stir regularly until the bechamel has thicken. Add salt, pepper and nutmeg. Set aside.
  3. Butter the outside of the slices of bread.
  4. On one slice, spread mustard, add the ham and a slice of cheese.
  5. Add the other slice on top. Spread a generous amount of bechamel. Add grated cheese to taste.
  6. Put in the oven for 10 min.
  7. Serve hot with lettuce on the side. Enjoy!
